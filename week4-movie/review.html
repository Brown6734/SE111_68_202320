Chloe Brown

Feburary, 7th, 2023

The movie is called The Greatest Showman. The movie was released in 2017. I think that the movie was good. It was better than I expected it to be.

This movie is a good family night movie. I believe that the actors did a job portraying the characters. The storytelling is good and the visuals are stunning.

Trivia

  • Hugh Jackman read some three dozen books on P.T. Barnum to prepare for the title role.
  • This film was a dream project for Hugh Jacman since 2009.
  • Many of the costumes used in the ensemble circus scenes at the beginning and end of the film wre borrowed from Feld Entertainment - the current owners of Ringling Brothers and Barnum & Bailey Circus. They were used in productions of "The Greatest Show on Earth"
